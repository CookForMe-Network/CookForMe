# CookForMe

**Home-Cooked Meals, Delivered to You**

A marketplace connecting talented home cooks with people who want authentic, home-cooked meals.

## What is CookForMe?

CookForMe brings the warmth of home cooking to your doorstep. Browse local cooks by cuisine, dietary preference, and availability, then book meals for pickup or delivery.

### For food lovers
- Discover home cooks in your area
- Filter by cuisine, dietary needs, and ratings
- Book meals on your schedule

### For home cooks
- Share your culinary skills and earn income
- Manage your menu, availability, and bookings
- Build a reputation through reviews
